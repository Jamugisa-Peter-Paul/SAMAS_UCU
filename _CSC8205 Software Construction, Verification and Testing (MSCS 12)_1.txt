QUESTION: CASE STUDY
You have been contracted by the Uganda Christian University (UCU) to design and develop a Smart Academic Management and Analytics System (SAMAS). The system aims to support the univeristy in managing student records, course delivery, assessments, and performance analytics in a centralized platform.
The proposed system must allow lecturers to create and manage courses, upload learning materials, design assessments, and track student progress. Students should be able to register for courses, submit assignments, take quizzes, and view their academic performance. Administrators should have access to dashboards that provide insights into course performance, student engagement, and institutional analytics.

Due to increasing demand for remote and hybrid learning, the system must support high availability, scalability, and security, ensuring that thousands of concurrent users can access the platform without performance degradation. Additionally, the system must integrate with third- party services such as email notification systems and authentication providers.
The university has emphasized that the system must be well-structured, maintainable, and extensible, as new features (e.g., AI-based recommendations, plagiarism detection, and mobile integration) will be added in future releases. Given the critical nature of academic data, the system must also be thoroughly tested, verified, and validated to ensure correctness, reliability, and security.
As the lead software engineer, you are required to design, develop, and present a high-quality, fully tested, and well-documented software solution, demonstrating best practices in software construction, verification, and testing.
